JOYFUL

A novel by Ingrid Fetell Lee that investigates how visual aesthetics in our day-to-day surroundings can contribute in massive ways to our overall happiness.

The liveliness of color helps us marshal the energy we need to learn, be productive, and grow. Bright color acts as a stimulant, a shot of caffeine for the eyes.
